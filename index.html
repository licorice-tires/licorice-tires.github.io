Licorice Tires

You’ve heard of licorice that tastes like tires, now get ready for tires that taste like licorice. If you are stuck on the side of the road with a flat, and you’re starting to feel famished, grab yourself a chunk of that tire and start to munch. Available in both the tire and food aisles of your local hardware store, Licorice Tires are a tasty treat on any warm, sunny day.
